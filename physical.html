Physical Pain


When dealing with physical pain, life becomes more complicated, and your day to day norms might become more difficult for you to complete. These tips and exercises could help get you back to do the things you love

Try these exercises that can help you


Here are some tips to reduce physical pain

- Essential oils, specifically peppermint oil, lavender oil, and eucalyptus oil - Better sitting posture - Less screen time to avoid exposure to blue light - Sitting/laying in a room to perform exercises - Cupping - Over the counter medications (ex. tylenol, advil, ibuprofen) - TENS unit - Elevating legs to increase bloodflow throughout body - Heat/ice - Bath oils - Rest your body - Take time for yourself - Don't exert yourself

Food tips that can help get your life back on track

- Eliminating processed/junk foods - Adding ingredients that are known to reduce inflammation like ginger, turmeric, camu camu powder, olive oil - Eat green and leafy vegetables that contain lots of fiber - Fruits such as strawberries, blueberries, cherries, and oranges - Try and avoid fried foods, and refined carbs
